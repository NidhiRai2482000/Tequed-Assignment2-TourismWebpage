Flight Reservation

As an IATA, ASATA and ANTA accredited travel agency Blueline offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made. Blueline’s association with Tourvest Travel Services gives Bliueline a firm representation in the international arena.

Accommodation

Blueline takes pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally. Certain corporate discounts are available, with preferred accommodation establishments (with bill back facilities).

Car Hire

Blueline is an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. A mutually beneficial relationship exists with Europcar, Budget and Avis from with special rates which are passed onto our customers. However, should the customer prefer any other car rental company, Blueline will oblige.

Visas

Blueline has a department which offers professional and timeous visa services. The department also advises clients regarding visa requirements and/or any other information relevant to a specific destination.

Emergency Services

Blueline provides a 24 hour, 7 day a week emergency service to all customers.
